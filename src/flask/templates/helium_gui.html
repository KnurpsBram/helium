Welcome to the Helium Web App!

It's a misconception that helium raises the pitch of your voice. It actually changes the speed of sound through the air. Your vocal cords create the same pitch, but the air in your mouth resonates at different frequencies! With this little webapp you can play with changing the pitch and formants of your voice independently (those resonance frequencies are called 'formants')
